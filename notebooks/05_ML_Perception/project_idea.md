## Machine-Learning in Perception & Control

**1. Notebook:** *Object detection fine-tuning (YOLOv5/v8) on small dataset*

* Tools: PyTorch, Ultralytics YOLO, CV notebooks
* Show metric (mAP), training logs, sample inference visualization.

**2. Project:** *End-to-end imitation learning for cart-pole balancing*

* Tools: Gymnasium, Stable-Baselines3, PyTorch
* Collect trajectory data from expert controller (MPC), train a supervised policy, compare performance.

**3. Project:** *NN-assisted MPC: learning a disturbance estimator*

* Tools: CasADi + PyTorch
* Demonstrate improved tracking when MPC uses learned disturbance model.

**Repo helpers:** PyTorch, TensorFlow, Stable-Baselines3, Ultralytics, OpenCV.
